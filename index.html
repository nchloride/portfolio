WORKS

FILI-ISIPAN

FILI-ISIPAN is an Android 3D Exploration Word Puzzle mobile game that is inspired by the National Language of the Philippines, Filipino. The player will act as Buboy, the protagonist. He was selected by the Mystery Stone to be the holder of its power where the stone will give him the power of manipulating all the letters of the Filipino alphabet and construct Filipino words unto them. FILI-ISIPAN will teach the players how to use their Critical Thinking skills by creating new words on a specific given set of words and on by this technique, the player will acquire better vocabulary by thinking and giving more broad ideas to be able to finish the game.
